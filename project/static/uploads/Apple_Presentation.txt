Apple Presentation

Introduction

Apple Inc. is one of the world’s leading technology companies, known for
its innovative products, software, and services.

Company Overview

-   Founded: April 1, 1976
-   Founders: Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne
-   Headquarters: Cupertino, California, USA

Popular Products

-   iPhone
-   iPad
-   MacBook
-   Apple Watch
-   AirPods

Services

-   Apple Music
-   iCloud
-   Apple TV+
-   Apple Arcade
-   App Store

Key Innovations

-   Touchscreen smartphones
-   Custom Apple Silicon chips
-   Ecosystem integration across devices

Advantages

-   User-friendly design
-   Strong security and privacy features
-   High-quality hardware and software

Conclusion

Apple continues to shape the technology industry through innovation,
design excellence, and a seamless user experience.
